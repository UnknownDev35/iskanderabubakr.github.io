Iskander Abubakr is an entrepreneur and investor driven by a singular conviction: that the most meaningful outcomes emerge at the crossroads of bold ideas and disciplined execution.

Through ventures, investments, and content, he explores what it means to build lasting value — whether in business, markets, or culture. As a pianist, he brings the same focus and discipline to music as he does to the boardroom.

Based across international hubs, Iskander works with founders, investors, and creators who share an obsession with doing things differently.
